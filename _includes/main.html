Curriculum Vitae

I am a sixth year PhD student in the COMMIT group at MIT CSAIL and advised by Prof. Saman Amarasinghe and Prof. Julian Shun.

My research interests lie primarily in the area of perfomrance optimizations, programming languages and compilers for graph analytics and machine learning. I have developed the GraphIt domain-specific language and compiler for writing high-performance graph analytics applications. I have also worked on cache optimizations for graph analytics and sparse linear algebra.
